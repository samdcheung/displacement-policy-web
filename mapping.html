Governance And Fiscal Architecture Diagnostic

Displacement Governance Mapping is a comparative diagnostic framework examining how governance systems absorb displacement-related pressures.

Rather than tracking displaced populations, it identifies where governance systems fragment, where fiscal architecture constrains inclusion, and what reforms can support sustainable inclusion.

The framework focuses on governance fault lines, institutional absorption capacity, fiscal architecture, and reform opportunities across displacement contexts.

Methodology Note

Diagnostic, Not Caseload Tracking

The mapping framework assesses categorical, temporal, territorial, fiscal, and infrastructure fault lines. Ratings are comparative diagnostics intended to identify institutional constraints and reform pathways, not to count displaced populations.
