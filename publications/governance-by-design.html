Governance by Design

The displacement continuum and sustainable inclusion in Southeast Asia.

Author
Samuel Cheung
Publication type
Policy Brief
Publication date
June 2026

Abstract

This policy brief introduces the displacement continuum as a framework for understanding how responsibility for displaced populations shifts across legal categories, institutions, territories, and time horizons, with attention to sustainable inclusion in Southeast Asia.
