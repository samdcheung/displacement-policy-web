Beyond Shock-Responsiveness

Horizontal integration as the next frontier of adaptive social protection.

Abstract

This policy brief argues that adaptive social protection must move beyond temporary shock-response triggers toward horizontal integration across registries, services, financing channels, and displacement governance systems.
