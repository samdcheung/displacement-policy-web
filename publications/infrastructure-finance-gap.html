The Infrastructure Finance Gap

Development-induced displacement and the limits of project-based safeguards.

Author
Samuel Cheung
Publication type
Policy Brief
Publication date
June 2026

Abstract

This policy brief examines development-induced displacement and the limits of project-based safeguards, focusing on how infrastructure finance can generate long-term public obligations that outlast project closure.
