About

A research platform examining displacement through the lens of governance.

Displacement Policy bridges humanitarian policy, development policy, and public administration to explore how responsibility for displaced populations is organised across laws, institutions, public systems, and financing arrangements.

Research focuses on governance architecture, institutional adaptation, social protection, fiscal systems, and pathways to sustainable inclusion.
