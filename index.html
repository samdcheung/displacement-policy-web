Southeast Asia Project

Displacement Governance Mapping

Displacement Governance Mapping is a comparative project assessing how Southeast Asian states govern displacement through legal frameworks, institutional mandates, social protection systems, fiscal architecture, and development finance exposure.

The project identifies where ordinary public systems absorb displacement-related vulnerability — and where responsibility breaks down.
