ABOUT

An independent research platform on the governance of displacement.

Displacement Policy examines how responsibility for displaced populations is organised across institutions, public systems and financing arrangements — and why protection and solutions often become fragmented as people move across legal categories, territories and systems.

Its research connects displacement with public policy, institutional design, political economy and finance to explore how responsibility can be sustained more effectively over time.

RESEARCH AGENDA

Research on how displacement is governed.

The research agenda examines how responsibility for displaced populations is assigned, financed and sustained — and where institutional continuity breaks down across public systems, jurisdictions and time.
