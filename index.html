Platform

Displacement governance treated as ordinary public responsibility.

Displacement Policy is an independent research platform focused on the governance systems that shape protection, inclusion, and solutions for displaced populations. It examines displacement not only as a humanitarian concern, but as a test of public institutions, fiscal architecture, development finance, and state capacity.

The platform focuses on how displacement-related responsibilities are absorbed into ordinary systems, including budgets, social protection, infrastructure planning, civil registration, development lending, and regional governance, and where those systems fail to carry responsibility over time.
